Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Data We Collect

Spendly collects the personal expense information you choose to enter, such as transaction amounts, categories, dates, and optional descriptions ("Expense Data"). We also store the basic account details you provide to register, such as your email address and a securely hashed copy of your password. We do not require or collect sensitive financial credentials such as full bank login details or complete payment card numbers.

How We Use Your Data

Your Expense Data is used solely to provide the Service — to display your spending, generate summaries, and let you manage your records. We use your email address to authenticate your account and communicate important service updates. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your Expense Data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on servers associated with the Service and protected using reasonable technical and organizational measures. Expense Data is kept for as long as your account is active; you may delete individual records at any time, and you can request deletion of your account and associated data. While we work to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party providers for hosting, analytics, or infrastructure. These providers are given access only to the information needed to perform their function on our behalf and are expected to handle it in line with this policy. We do not share your Expense Data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond to reasonable requests and work with you to address any concerns about your personal information.
